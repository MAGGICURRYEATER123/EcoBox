Core Technologies

Arduino Uno

The reliable brain for core control — powering your relay and sensors.

ESP32

Handles WiFi + Bluetooth + logic, streaming data via Blynk dashboard.

DHT11 Sensor

Monitors temperature and humidity with precision for indoor control.

YL-69 Soil Sensor

Detects moisture level in soil for intelligent water spray decisions.

Relay Module

Turns pump and fan on/off safely via commands from the controller.

DC Fan & Pump

Automated air cooling and water control based on real-time data.
