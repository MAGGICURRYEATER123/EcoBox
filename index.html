EcoBox

Innovation in smart climate control, sustainable hydration, and efficient environmental monitoring.

Core Technologies

ESP32
The powerful brain running all smart controls with ultra-efficient processing.
Arduino Uno
Reliable microcontroller handling sensor data and device management.
DHT11 Sensor
Measures temperature and humidity for precise climate monitoring.
Soil Moisture Sensor
Detects soil hydration to prevent overwatering and conserve water.
Fan
Circulates air to maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels.
Water Pump
Delivers precise irrigation controlled by moisture readings.
